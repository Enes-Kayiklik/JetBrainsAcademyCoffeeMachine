Triangle Open on JetBrains Academy

There are three natural numbers A, B, C. Define if a triangle with such sides exists.

If the triangle exists, output the YES string, otherwise output NO.

Note that a triangle is formed by three connected points that are not located on a single straight line.


Sample Input:
3
4
5
Sample Output:
YES


Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 1 second
